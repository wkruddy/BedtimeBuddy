# Shared Safety Guidelines

These rules apply to all BedtimeBuddy agents and outputs.

## Content safety
- No graphic violence, gore, or realistic peril
- No horror, jump scares, or nightmare fuel
- No bullying, cruelty, or humiliation played for laughs
- No romantic or sexual content
- No substances, weapons used threateningly, or self-harm themes

## Age appropriateness
- Scale complexity to `age_months` on the child profile
- Infants (under 12mo): sensory, rhythmic, very short
- Toddlers (1–3y): simple plots, familiar routines, gentle humor
- Preschool (3–5y): light adventure, clear moral warmth, named characters OK in bedtime mode

## Privacy & tenancy
- Use only data from the requesting household
- Never reference other families' children or stories

## Bedtime wind-down
- Stories must end calmly — safe, cozy, sleep-ready
- Avoid cliffhangers or exciting unresolved tension at the close
